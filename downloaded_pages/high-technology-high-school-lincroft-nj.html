    During my time at High Tech, I was immersed in science and technology enriched courses. Students are able to build their engineering skills and become more independent. However, this school does not offer a lot for more arts/humanities based students. There is no art class in the curriculum. This is something that I would like to see changed. The school does a great job at preparing students for engineering, but adding an art class, or something arts based would help to make the students more well rounded.

    Review High Technology High School
    Rating 3 out of 5  
    Everyone sees the rankings of this school and wants to attend. It's a great education if you want to go deep into technology but it leaves a lot to be desired. Many of the classes that matter are actually hit-or-miss. There is a ton of pressure -a TON - which I personally like. The environment is collaborative if you are part of the culture that invites you in. There are a ton of geeks here but within that, there's a hierarchy. It feels very much like a caste system. Will you learn a ton? Possibly, it's all about what you put in. Will you get into an Ivy league? Probably not. The admissions process for colleges are strange and many people from regular public schools get in before kids from here. It's true. So if you want to attend to learn, then go here. If you want a more well-rounded experience and a chance to get into your top school - go to a regular public school, do extremely well and you'll probably have a shot.
